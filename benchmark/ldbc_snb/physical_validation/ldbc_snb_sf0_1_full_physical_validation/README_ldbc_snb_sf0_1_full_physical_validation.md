# LDBC SNB SF0.1 full physical MongoDB validation

This folder consolidates the pilot physical validation for all LDBC SNB official queries used in the SchemaLens experiment at SF0.1.

## Scope

The validation covers:

- IC1–IC7 interactive complex read queries
- IS1–IS7 interactive short read queries
- INS1–INS8 insert/update workloads

In total, this phase validates 22 official LDBC SNB queries over the activated physical MongoDB candidate families generated by SchemaLens.

## Purpose

The goal is to validate the faithful physical MongoDB materializations before larger benchmark campaigns.

The validation checks:

1. candidate execution success;
2. semantic equivalence for read workloads;
3. physical-maintenance correctness for write workloads;
4. query-plan or write-path evidence;
5. absence of collection scans in inspected read plans;
6. successful document writes for INS workloads.

## Files

- `ldbc_snb_sf0_1_physical_validation_summary.csv`
- `ldbc_snb_sf0_1_physical_validation_global_summary.csv`

## Notes

IC and IS are read-oriented workloads. They are validated through semantic equivalence across candidate families and query-plan evidence.

INS workloads are write-oriented. They are validated through successful writes and physical-maintenance checks over base and derived candidate-specific structures.
